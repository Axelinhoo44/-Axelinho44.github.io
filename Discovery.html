Discovery Phase

The Discovery phase of a company consists in the discovery and study of the target it addresses, thanks to community analysis and targeted surveys. With the collected data it is possible to create personas that emulate the typical behavior of a possible user. By tracking user journeys, it is possible to understand the site's pain points by revisiting the information hierarchy and the various pages. We can call it a redesign of the site, to make it efficient for its target.
